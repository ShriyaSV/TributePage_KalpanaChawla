Kalpana Chawla

Astronaut Kalpana Chawla image

Information of Kalpana Chawla:

• 1961 - Kalpana Chawla was born on March 17 in Karnal, Haryana, India.
• 1982 - Immigrated to the United States to pursue her dreams in aerospace engineering.
• 1988 - Earned a Master's degree in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Texas at Arlington.
• 1994 - Completed a second Master's degree, this time in Mechanical Engineering, from the University of Texas at Arlington.
• 1997 - On November 19, became the first woman of Indian origin in space aboard the Space Shuttle Columbia (Mission: STS-87).
• 2003 - Tragically lost her life on February 1, when the Space Shuttle Columbia disintegrated during re-entry.
• Posthumously - Kalpana Chawla was posthumously awarded the Congressional Space Medal of Honor, the NASA Space Flight Medal, and several other honors.
